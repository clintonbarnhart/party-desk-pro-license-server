- License operations KPI cards
- Advanced license, event, and text filters

## Test checklist

1. Open Party Desk Pro > License Activity and confirm metric cards load.
2. Test license, event type, and search filters.
3. Activate a client site and confirm it appears in Authorized Websites.
4. Sign into `[pdpclientportal]` and confirm authorized sites and recent activity appear.
